Low Poly Pack

hl_models_lp_2025-09-18.7z (8,152 models, 636 MB download, 5.9 GB extracted)

This pack excludes models with a polygon count above 1500. Old versions of models are also excluded.

1500 was chosen to limit the total player polys in a server to about 100k (32 players + 32 corpses). Performance issues begin at around 50k on modern hardware but it should be uncommon to see 32 players/corpses in one area, as well as for all players to choose a 1500 poly model.

Installation

Extract either pack to valve_downloads, found here:
Steam/steamapps/common/Half-Life/valve_downloads/

You may not want to extract the sound folder. Model sounds sometimes trigger rapidly and drown out every other sound in the game.
